Unearthingabrighter tomorrow


The Government of India has opened up mining to the private sector. NCC has ventured into this sphere to play a catalytic role in making India more self reliant in mining natural resources.

To address the widening gap between demand and supply of coal and the increased demand for power, Government of India has brought out some reforms in the mining sector viz. participation and permission for commercial mining etc., creating greater opportunities for investment and growth in mining.

Our mining division focuses on businesses for Mine Developer ofk-cum-Operator (MDO), removal of overburden and the extraction coal / lignite / other minerals from open cast mines in India.
