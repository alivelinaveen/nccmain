Increasing operational coverage

NCC Limited has made a mark in the country as one of the largest listed construction companies in terms of revenue, with well diversified activities comprising buildings, transportation, water & environment, irrigation, electrical (T&D), mining and railways. The group has expanded its footprints to have a pan India presence with offices across 9 cities in Ahmedabad, Bengaluru, Chennai, Delhi, Lucknow, Mumbai, Pune, Kolkata, and Patna with headquarters located at Hyderabad.
