---WATER & ENVIRONMENT


Deep in innovation


With the phenomenal andincessant growth in population, urbanisation and
rapid industrialisation, the need for water has overtaken its supply.

We have made our presence felt through significant water and sanitation
projects-including the unique Limca Book of Records entry, the 820 kms.’
gravity-based water supply system in Amaravati, Maharashtra. Over the next
few years, water supply&sanitation projects are expected to witness
significant outlays.Our experience and excellent credentials will help us cruise
through this space successfully.

Management of water and waste water has assumed enormous significance
and NCC plays a crucial role in this field. NCC has augmented its expertise to be
able to execute various water supply systems, drainage systems and sewage
treatment plants through specialised systems for high value projects.

A feather in our cap


Listed in the Limca Book of Records for the 820 kms.gravity-based water supply.system in Amaravati, Maharashtra.

Some of the key projects in this sector include

  • Construction of intake PS at river Chambal near Bhaisrodgarh, 3 nos. IPS & RWTM from intake to WTP under Chambal-Bhilwara Water Supply Project Phase-I with operation and maintenance on single point responsibility

  • Construction of 245 MLD capacity WTP at Rasadpura (Aroli) and MS clear water gravity main from WTP to transfer pumping station at Bhilwara city with operation and maintenance for 10 years on single point responsibility turnkey basis

  • Sauni Yojna Link 3, Package 1-EPC contract for construction of PS at Wadhwan Bhogavo II Reservoir and supplying & laying of twin MS pipeline (pumping) of 3000 mm dia (ID) 17.5 mm thick from PS to Chainage 12.7 km including operation and maintenance for 10 years

  • Sauni Yojna Link 3, Package 2-EPC contract for construction of IPS at Chainage 12.7 km near Khatadi village and supplying & laying of twin MS pipeline (pumping) of 3000 mm dia (ID) 17.5 mm thick from PS to CH 41.8 km including operation and maintenance for 10 years

  • Sauni Yojna - Phase: II, Link - 4, Package - 5 (A)-EPC contract for construction of pumping station at Ch. 15.340 km near Village Khambhala and supplying & laying MS Pipeline of 2850mm / 2600mm / 1000mm dia (ID) from Ch. 0.0 km to Ch. 41.450 km & gravity feeder pipe line (1.590 km) with feeder pumping station including operation and maintenance for 10 years

  • Sauni Yojna - Phase: II, Link - 4, Package - 6 - EPC contract for construction of pumping station at Ch. 59.980 km near Village Lakhapadar and supplying & laying MS Pipeline of 3000 mm / 2700 mm (ID) from Ch. 59.980 km to Ch. 111.129 km & gravity feeder pipe line (42.019 km) & li feeder pipe line (6.103 km) with FPS including operation and maintenance for10 years

  • Providing drinking water to Chevella, Vikarabad, Pargi, Tandur and Maheshwaram constituencies in Rangareddy from Srisailam Reservoir under Telangana Drinking Water Supply Project (TDWSP) including operation and maintenance for 10 Years under Telangana Drinking Water Supply Project (TDWSP)
  • Providing drinking water supply to habitations in HMWSSB segment in Warangal district under Telangana Drinking Water Supply Project

  • Water supply Distribution network project for the peripheral circles of GHMC - construction of RCC service reservoirs and providing inlet, outlet & Distribution network in Quthubullapur, L.B. Nagar, Gaddiannaram, Rajendranagar Circles – Package-II

  • Distribution network improvements for NRW reduction and 24x7 water supply in northwest sector of Greater Visakhapatnam Municipal Corporation

  • Balance Work of Cluster Scheme of 315 Villages of Mandalgarh, Bijoliya andJahajpur tehsils along with augmentation of UWSS of Mandalgarh and Bijoliyatowns from Aroli headworks under Chambal-Bhilwara water supply projectPhase – II, Rajasthan with operation and maintenance for 10 years on point responsibility turnkey basis
