---Irrigation


Sowingtheseedsof future



The lifeline of farmers and the basic requirement to feed the vast population, irrigation infrastructure includes a network of major, medium and minor canals from rivers, ground water, well-based systems, tanks, and other rainwater harvesting projects for agricultural activities. Other activities that are predominantly dependent on the availability of water resources are forestry, animal husbandry, horticulture and sericulture. NCC plays a crucial role in improving food security, reducing dependence on monsoons, improving agricultural & allied industrial productivity and creating jobs in the rural areas through its wide expertise and resources in irrigation infrastructure development projects.
